Happy Republic Day

Republic Day of India is celebrated every year on 26 January to mark the day when the Constitution of India came into effect in 1950, officially making India a sovereign, democratic republic.

  • Republic Day is celebrated on 26 January every year because the Constitution of India came into force on this day in 1950.
  • Dr. B. R. Ambedkar is known as the chief architect of the Indian Constitution.
  • The first Republic Day parade was held in 1950 at Rajpath (now Kartavya Path), New Delhi.
